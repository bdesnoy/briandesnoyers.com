Design your own study plan in minutes.

StudyPlanner is designed to make it easy to generate study plans from lists of resources. Simply add the resources you want to use, then send them to your todo list for scheduling.

iCloud Sync

Keep your schedule in sync across iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and Mac!

Flexible

Easily import new study resources from CSV files or create them in the app.

Easy Access

Keep track of your schedule at any time from a home screen widget or watch complication.

Simple

A simple design makes schedule changes fast and easy.

Take back control of your schedule

StudyPlanner was built by a medical student studying for board exams who was sick of using school-provided and service-generated schedules. StudyPlanner provides a lightweight way for you to create your schedule, because you know you best.
