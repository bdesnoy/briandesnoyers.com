Projects

Bike Beacon I have developed a novel cyclist-driver communication application to be used by cyclists and vehicle drivers to prevent unnecessary collisions. For more information, please visit bikebeacon.com. Based on input from our buisness advisors, our team has moved to a hardware model to improve user adoption. I am currently working on designing software to run directly on our hardware sensors and transmitters that we hope to market shortly.
iOS Psychomotor Vigilance Task I have developed a millisecond accurate iOS PVT along with a cognitive testing platform for Brigham and Women’s Hospital during my co-op working as a technician in their sleep laboratory. The PVT is a sustained attention test that has been shown to respond to changes in alertness caused by factors such as sleep deprivation and circadian phase. I am currently writing a technical paper about the approaches used to accomplish this task and improve iPhone and iPad response time accuracy.
Swim Team Web Application I have designed a web application for swim team management that allows swimmers and parents to sign up for meets and view and analyze their times, among other things. The software imports and exports to proprietary formats utilized by commonly used desktop swim and meet management software and allows coaches to easily manage their teams through a simple web interface. I have also designed a companion iOS app and web API. A running instance of the software can be viewed at streamline.pythonanywhere.com.
